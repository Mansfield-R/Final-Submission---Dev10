Who We Are:

Americana captures the imagination of the United States of America by building on the traditions of the 1950's generation. We want to recreate every aspect of America's old dining experience including: the energy, friendliness of staff, classic upbeat music, and everything in-between. Expect second-to-none customer service and a quality in all of our dishes that will keep every Americana customer that tries us once to come back for a second time.
